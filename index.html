Hi, I'm MyCMS

A Great Open Source CMS

Templating

MyCMS come with 2 templates engine. The latest version supports templates powered by Twig, an easy to understand templating language written specifically for designers.

Responsive

MyADMIN the management panel is responsive, whether on the go on your mobile device, or at home on your laptop or desktop, to easily manage your website in whatever place you are.

Content

If you don't know how write the code of the page of your website or simply you don't want write code, you can build your website with an intuitive editor powered by tinymce.

Blog

Another functionality is the blog creation, if you want have a blog on your website, MyCMS can help you.

User Management

If you are a team and you want use MyCMS, you can easily manage all people.

Developers

Are you a developer? You want build your own theme or make your website easily, MyCMS have a lot of functionality for build from your own pc or online.
